# ESP32_12864-Smart-LCD-Controller
Interfacing ESP32 with RepRapDiscount Full Graphic Smart Controller

## Pinout :
### EXP1

      10 | +5V   |  GND  | 09
      08 |  NC   |   NC  | 07
      06 |  NC   | LCD-E | 05
      04 |LDC-RS |LCD-R/W| 03
      02 |ENC-BTN|  BUZ  | 01

      10 |  NC   |   NC  | 09
      08 |  BTN  |   NC  | 07

      04 |  NC   | ENC-B | 03
      02 |  NC   |   NC  | 01
